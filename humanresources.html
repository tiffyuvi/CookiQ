Humanities Subjects

Dive deep into the world of humanities with these resources for writing, research, and critical thinking.

  • History.com - Explore historical events and resources for social studies.
  • The Economist - Stay updated with global economic trends and analyses.
  • Khan Academy Humanities - Lessons on history, economics, and art.
  • Shmoop - Study guides for literature, social studies, and economics.
  • SparkNotes - Literature summaries, essay ideas, and study guides.
  • LibGuides - Research guides and resources for academic projects.
  • Our World in Data - Data and research on global social and economic trends.
  • TED - Inspirational talks on history, economics, and humanities topics.
  • Encyclopedia Britannica - Reliable reference for social studies and humanities research.
  • ResearchGate - Access academic papers and connect with researchers.
  • UNC Writing Center - Detailed guides on essay writing and critical analysis.
