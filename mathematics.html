Mathematics

Math is like a language and art form that requires immersion and consistent practice. Key learning principles, like the Testing Effect (active recall), Spacing Effect (revisiting concepts over time), and Interleaving (mixing different problem types), help deepen understanding. By breaking down complex problems and practicing regularly, you can wield math as a powerful tool, much like an artist with their brush, to create solutions and explore new possibilities. Unlock the power of numbers with these resources for solving equations, visualizing concepts, and mastering problem-solving techniques.

  • Khan Academy - Comprehensive lessons on a wide range of math topics.
  • Desmos - Interactive graphing calculator and visualization tool.
  • BC Math Free lessons and learning materials aligned with the BC curriculum.
  • Wolfram Alpha - Powerful tool for solving equations and exploring mathematical concepts.
  • Mathway - Step-by-step solutions to math problems.
  • GeoGebra - Dynamic tools for geometry, algebra, and calculus.
  • Brilliant - Interactive problem-solving and concept mastery.
  • Art of Problem Solving - Advanced problems and solutions for math enthusiasts.
  • Math Is Fun - Accessible explanations for students of all levels.
  • Socratic - App and website that explains math problems using AI.
  • NRICH - Enriching mathematics problems and challenges.
  • CK-12 Math - Interactive lessons, practice problems, and concept builders.
