Computer Science & Technology

Computer science is the study of algorithms, programming, data structures, and computational theories. As a field, it focuses on solving problems through coding and applying logic to create systems, tools, and technologies that drive innovation in almost every aspect of life. To excel in coding, psychological principles like deliberate practice, spaced repetition, and the growth mindset can significantly enhance learning. By breaking down complex problems, focusing on incremental progress, and embracing challenges, you can build strong coding skills.
