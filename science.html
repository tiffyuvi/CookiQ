Science Subjects

Science subjects are challenging because they require building connections and thinking analytically. A key psychological principle is the testing effect, which shows that actively recalling information strengthens memory more than passive review. Famous physicist Richard Feynman often emphasized the importance of teaching concepts to truly understand them, a technique that encourages making connections and analyzing ideas deeply. These are the perfect tools for honing your analytical and reasoning skills!

  • Ptable Interactive and informative digital periodic table.
  • Khan Academy Comprehensive lessons in various sciences.
  • PhET Simulations Science simulations for physics and chemistry.
  • Chemguide Comprehensive resources for chemistry students.
  • BioInteractive Free biology videos and activities from HHMI.
  • The Physics Classroom Tutorials and problem-solving for physics.
  • Bozeman Science Engaging science videos and tutorials.
