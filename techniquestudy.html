Improve Your Study Technique

Stock up on resources to study smarter, not harder.

Research indicates that active learning methods lead to improved academic performance and better long-term retention compared to passive study techniques (such as rereading passages or highlighting).
To support your active studying efforts, the following resources offer platforms and tools designed to enhance your learning experience and help you COOK!

  • Knowt - Interactive flashcards with spaced repetition.
  • Quizlet - Flashcards, quizzes, and study sets for all subjects.
  • Remnote - Create notes and flashcards for better recall.
