Financial Literacy & Business

Financial literacy involves understanding the basics of money management, investing, and entrepreneurship, enabling individuals to make informed decisions about their personal finances and business ventures. Psychological principles like goal setting, delayed gratification, and self-discipline play a crucial role in managing finances effectively. By learning from failures and focusing on long-term financial planning, you can build financial security and success. Here are some resources to help you on your journey!

  • Investopedia - Learn about personal finance, investing, and business concepts.
  • Khan Academy Personal Finance - Free lessons on saving, budgeting, and financial literacy.
  • Coursera Business Courses - Online courses in finance, marketing, and management.
  • Mint - Budgeting and money management made simple.
  • YNAB - "You Need A Budget" helps you plan and track spending effectively.
  • edX Business and Finance - Free and paid courses from top universities.
  • Harvard Business Review - Articles and case studies on business trends and strategies.
  • Notion Business Templates - Tools for organizing business plans and workflows.
  • FinTeachable - Resources for understanding financial concepts and entrepreneurship.
  • Bplans - Business planning templates and advice for startups.
  • TED Business Talks - Inspirational and practical talks on leadership, innovation, and economics.
  • Simple - Tools and advice for personal banking and finance.
  • FutureLearn Business Courses - Free and premium courses in business and management.
